Buttermilk Fried Chicken

Description

In this recipe, you will learn how to make a southern classic... Buttermilk Fried Chicken! Few things are as tasty and rewarding as fried chicken.

There aren't many ingredients, but the key to this recipe is Buttermilk. Buttermilk is a great marinade for chicken and allows for a wonderful juicy and crunchy fried chicken.

Ingredients

  • 1(3lb) frying chicken cut into its 8 individual pieces
  • 2 cups Buttermilk
  • 1tsp garlic powder
  • 1tsp onion powder
  • 1tsp poultry seasoning
  • 1tsp celery seeds
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • 2cups Kikkoman Tempura Batter Mix
